About Us

To that place: Love

Across the ocean

There is a place

Where there is no one

To judge and criticize


Sunshine shine with warmth

Air breezes with intimacy

Flowers scent endearment

Love is everywhere


Let’s sail there

Let's sail there for love


Come closer to me

Let’s be fearless

Listen to my heartbeat

It leads to that place


Let’s go to that place

Where there is no time

Let’s spend our eternity there

And forget our painful distances
